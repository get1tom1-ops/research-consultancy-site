About Our Consultancy

Our Mission

Niguse Tadele Health Consultancy Service delivers evidence-based research, evaluation, and capacity building to support health system strengthening in Ethiopia. We work with government institutions, NGOs, and international partners to generate actionable insights that drive program improvement and policy decisions.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our research and engagements.

Quality

We deliver rigorous, evidence-based outputs that meet international standards.

Collaboration

We partner with stakeholders and communities to ensure locally relevant solutions.

Impact

We focus on generating insights that lead to tangible improvements in health outcomes.

Our Services

Health Research & Evaluation

Comprehensive study design, implementation, and analysis for public health programs and interventions.

Monitoring & Evaluation Systems

Development and implementation of robust M&E frameworks, tools, and data quality assurance protocols.

Survey Design & Implementation

Nationwide and sub-national surveys using digital data collection platforms (ODK, SurveyCTO, KoboToolbox).

Capacity Building & Training

Workshops and mentorship on research methods, M&E, data analysis, and evidence use for decision-making.

Program Design & Technical Assistance

Support for health program planning, proposal development, and implementation strategies.

Policy Analysis & Advocacy

Evidence synthesis and policy briefs to inform health policy and strategic planning.
